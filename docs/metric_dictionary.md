# Metric Dictionary

## churned
Binary target: 1 if the customer churned, 0 otherwise.

## churn_rate
Average of churned for a group of customers.

## revenue_at_risk
monthly_fee for churned customers. Used to quantify the commercial impact of churn.

## ticket_rate
support_tickets divided by tenure_months. Helps compare support friction across customers with different tenure.

## low_usage_flag
Indicator for customers below median usage hours.

## inactive_flag
Indicator for customers with last_login_days greater than or equal to 14.

## churn_risk_score
Predicted probability of churn generated by the logistic regression pipeline.

## retention_priority
churn_risk_score multiplied by monthly_fee. Used to prioritize outreach by risk and value.
