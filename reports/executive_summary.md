# Executive Summary

## Recommendation

Prioritize retention outreach for monthly-contract customers with low usage, high support volume, weak NPS, and high monthly fees. The largest immediate opportunity is not only reducing churn rate, but protecting high-value recurring revenue.

## Key Insights

- Monthly contracts show higher churn exposure than annual contracts.
- Support friction and inactivity are practical leading indicators for churn risk.
- NPS provides an interpretable signal that can be used in customer success workflows.
- A retention priority score helps focus outreach on customers where intervention is financially meaningful.

## Portfolio Narrative

This project demonstrates how a data analyst can combine segmentation, SQL, feature engineering, classification modeling, and executive communication to support retention strategy.
